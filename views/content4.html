What is Organic Traffic?

defined as visitors coming from a certain search engine , two example of Search engines are Google or Bing. Every paid search ads are not included in this category. All Organic search traffic is labeled in color GREEN. In the analaytics tool , if you see "UNKNOWN" or "SSL" , this means that the search terms are being contained in the data set. This is not because Google is not sharing this ifnnformation.

SEO in involved in Organic traffic. All blogs that blog consistently will have a steady increase in organic search traffic. It is important to look at every keywords and identify latest ranking opportunities every month

Traffic data is the best for your website and marketing initiatives.Everytime you write or promote your blog , you must use this information to track result and correlate these efforts to actual R.O.I. You must look at traffic numbers over long-term intervals so you will see the trends and reports on improvement.
